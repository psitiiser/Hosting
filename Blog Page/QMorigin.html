How Did Quantum Mechanics Revolutionize Our Understanding of Reality?



Neelabh, Biology MSC24

What if the world is far more peculiar than we ever imagined, with its smallest components governed by laws that defy intuition and reason? For centuries, scientists believed they had a firm grasp on the workings of the universe, explaining everything from planetary motion to the behaviour of gases. Yet, as they dug deeper into the microscopic world, an unsettling truth began to emerge: the universe doesn't behave as neatly as Newtonian physics suggested. Thus entered quantum mechanics, a theory that not only rewrote the rules but did so in a way that left even the greatest minds of the time scratching their heads.
