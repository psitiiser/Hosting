The Physics Society of IISER Thiruvananthapuram is delighted to present the inaugural edition of Entangled.

Physics is often described through equations, experiments, and discoveries. Yet behind all of these lies something more fundamental: curiosity. Entangled was created as a space to celebrate that curiosity, a collection of stories, ideas, reflections, interviews, projects, artwork, and explorations that showcase the many ways we engage with science.

In this first issue, you will find articles spanning astrophysics, biophysics, quantum phenomena, scientific communication, student projects, science fiction, research journeys, and much more. Written and curated by students, faculty, and members of our community, the magazine reflects the diverse interests and perspectives that make science vibrant and exciting.

 

Front and Back Covers of Entangled Vol. 1

Whether you are a physics enthusiast, a student from another discipline, or simply someone who enjoys learning something new, we hope you will find something within these pages that sparks your curiosity.
