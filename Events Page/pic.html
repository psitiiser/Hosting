Photography Contest: Foundation Week Special

Welcome to the PSI(t) Physics Photography Contest, where we celebrate the beauty of science through your lenses! Foundation Week is all about exploring the wonders of physics, and we want you to capture it creatively.

Unleash Your Creativity with Physics!

Physics is not just in textbooks; it's all around us, from the ripples on a pond to the twinkling stars. It's time to showcase your unique perspective and find the beauty in everyday physics.

How to Participate:

  1. Grab your camera or smartphone and start exploring. Find that perfect physics moment.
  2. Capture a photograph that highlights the magic of physics. Get creative!
  3. Share your entry on Instagram Stories, making sure to tag @psitiiser.
  4. Direct Message (DM) the photo to our Instagram account.

Guidelines for Participants:

  • Format: Choose landscape, portrait, or square - it's your call!
  • Keep it Real: Minor adjustments are okay, but no heavy editing or Photoshop.
  • Location: Anywhere on Earth is fair game. Get creative with your surroundings.
  • Originality Counts: No plagiarism or AI-generated images. We value your unique vision.

Winners will not only receive exciting prizes but will also have their photographs featured on our official PSI(t) Instagram page. It's a chance to showcase your talent and love for physics to the world!

Foundation Week is the perfect time to blend art and science. Let's celebrate physics through your lens. We can't wait to see what you capture!

Happy clicking, and may the best photographer win!
