Celebrate the 5th Anniversary of PSI(T)



-The Physics Society of IISER Thiruvananthapuram (PSI(T))

Get ready to celebrate the 5th anniversary of The Physics Society of IISER Thiruvananthapuram (PSI(T)) in style!

In addition to engaging talks, fun competitions, and quizzes, this year's celebration will also feature a Poster Presentation Competition. This is a great opportunity for students to showcase their research interests and beloved physics concepts in a creative and interactive way.

Here are the details:

  • Who can participate: Undergraduate students, BSMS students, M.Sc. students, iPh.D. students, and PhD students
  • Poster size: A0 (33.1 in x 46.8 in) or A1 (23.4 in x 33.1 in) - landscape or portrait format (A1 preferred)
  • Individual or team presentations: Both are allowed
  • Abstract: Submit a short abstract (maximum 200 words) of your proposed topic in the registration form
  • Registration deadline: January 15, 2025 (The responses can be edited until the deadline)
  • Printing: Participants are responsible for printing their own posters
  • Contact: psit@iisertvm.ac.in (Please include "PPT: Query on event" in the subject line)


Don't miss this chance to participate in a fun and enriching event!

We look forward to seeing you there!
